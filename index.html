TEDDYFEELS

Who are we?

A company that creates teddy bears for emotional development while promoting environmental awareness, using sustainable materials.

How does it work?

These teddy bears contain a chip system that transmits information to an app for parents or guardians. This enables them to understand their children’s real emotional states and help them feel happy and safe.

Our Products

Classic TeddyFeel

The traditional teddy that combines softness and technology for children’s emotional well-being.

Eco TeddyFeel

Made with 100% eco-friendly materials, promoting emotional well-being and environmental care.

Benefits of TeddyFeels

  • Emotional development: Helps children identify and manage their emotions in a healthy way.
  • Environmental awareness: Made with recyclable and sustainable materials.
  • Technological connection: Parents can monitor their children’s emotional state through an intuitive app.
